      • Scholarship 1
      Institute Merit-cum-Means(MCM) Scholarships(Undergraduate Programs):
      The Institute offers Merit-cum-Means scholarships to graduate students in engineering and technology.
      These scholarships are offered to about 25% of the students.The present value of Merit-cum-Means scholarshipis INR 1000/- per month for General/OBC/EWS students and the recipient is exempted from paying the tution fee.
      Scholarship 2
      Scholarship provision for SC,ST & PD students(Undergraduate Programs):
      Tution fee exemption is admissible to all SC/ST/PD students irrespective of their parents'/guardians' income,Institute offers several other benefits to students from these categories.
      For more details please visit: https://beb.iitd.ac.in/Prospectus-2021-2022.pdf
