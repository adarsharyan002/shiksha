What's new at Indian Institute of Technology Delhi?

IIT Delhi admission 2022 is now open for MTech, M.Des, M.P.P, M.Sc, MS by Research and PhD courses. Candidates seeking admission can apply online till April 11, 2022. For details, click here Meanwhile, JEE Main 2022 exam date for the April session has been rescheduled. The NTA will now conduct JEE Main 2022 April session exam on April 21, 24, 25, 29 and May 1 and 4 The exam date for the second session (May) has not been changed. Candidates can fill out the application form online for session one till March 31, 2022. Registration for JEE Main 2022 May session will start on April 8.
